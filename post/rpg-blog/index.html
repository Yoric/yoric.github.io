New blog: Space·Time Will Tell

rpg improv storytelling

I have decided to keep this blog for professional use and move everything related to storytelling, role-playing games or improv acting to a new location.

Without further ado, let me introduce Space·Time Will Tell!

At the menu:

  • a comic book tutorial for Fate RPG!
  • using Tarot-like cards for storytelling!
  • several elevator pitches for stories I haven’t had the time to write!
  • the Space·Time Deck!
  • the Ronin Time Bureaucrats!
  • adventure and excitement!

See you on the other side :)
